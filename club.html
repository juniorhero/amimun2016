AMIMUN Club

Welcome to the Amity International Model United Nations Club

My name is Satrajit Sahani and I have the honor of serving as the Chief Student Coordinator for Amity University’s Model UN platform. I have been a regular face in various Model UN conferences all around the country since 2009 and I hope along with your support we will embark on a truly memorable journey.

2015 is a truly special year as it marks the 70th anniversary of the founding of the United Nations, it is the year when nations will come together to revisit the goals that were set. Keeping the significance of the year 2015 in mind, Amity International Model United Nations Club seeks to explore the world of international diplomacy through the structured debates based on the United Nations. This club teaches its members the skills of diplomacy, as well as offers a platform to improve public speaking and debating skills. Additionally, AMIMUN encourages people to work together to figure out solutions to the world’s problems.

AMIMUN Club is more than a club, it is a potential congregation of the future leadership and it is for this reason I believe that AMIMUN Club will serve as a stepping stone for you to become part of something amazing, and something helpful. Through the workshops that we simulate and the issues that we will touch upon, I want you to reflect on your responsibilities towards our country and the world in general. We may have chosen to blur what’s happening around us, but the time has come to become part of the change this country needs. One that does not call for chants to be coined or irrational stances to be thrust upon, rather one that calls on each one of us to understand what we can do in our capacities and then act to reform India.

I hope to see you all in the club meetings to accomplish your goals and to embark on the vision that we have set.

I once again welcome you all on board.

Regards and warm wishes,

Satrajit Sahani

Chief Student Coordinator

More About AMIMUN CLUB

We are the MUN Club of Amity University a group of motivated and sincere students interested in the art of Diplomacy and the world of International Relations. We proudly represent Amity International Model United Nation (AMIMUN) conference which is one of the biggest MUNs in Delhi hosted and organised by Amity University. We work hard and train diligently to experience, exchange, negotiate, represent and learn all that there is.

Rich with practical experience and lead by seasoned MUNers, we strive to bring out the best in each member of this club and promote the basic values of the United Nations. The talent is there and the future clear: We want to continue our MUN journey into the future with more experience and motivation and belief in our fellow young student diplomats.

OBJECTIVES:

  • to tap the potential MUNers/debaters looking for a platform to make their voice heard
  • help the students nurture their skills
  • create a pool of MUNers for future such conferences
  • to involve interested students into the world of International politics and relations
  • offer support and workshops to shape the youth leaders of tomorrow
  • promote the concept of MUNs and engage more and more students in this activity

FUNCTIONS:

  • the MUN Club will offer training sessions which will regularly take place during the academic semester
  • to give newcomers a chance to get an insight into MUNing and to learn the procedures and basic skills that are needed to successfully participate in MUN conferences
  • to form a base of MUNers for future AMIMUN conferences both as delegates and secretariat members
  • to help the students participate in other MUN conferences by providing the required training
