From the Secretary General

Dear Prospective Participants,

I am honored to invite you to apply to the fifth edition of Amity International Model United Nations, AMIMUN'16 to be held from Friday, January 8, to Sunday, January 10, 2016 at Amity University, Uttar Pradesh.

An MUN conference is one of its kinds and what sets it apart is its ability to open the doors of international reconnection and association to the future generations who are ready to step in to the corridors of diplomacy and seize the moment to work towards becoming responsible leaders of tomorrow. The vast array of topics and committees will further ensure a uniquely engaging and thought-provoking experience for each and every delegate.

Dear Prospective Delegates, I strongly encourage you to take full advantages of the opportunity brought forward by AMIMUN. I encourage you to be creative, test the limits of current international relations, while remaining realistic, learn as much as you can from this experience and bear those lessons in mind when entrusted with real decision making power in your future careers.

AMIMUN is an exciting educational opportunity, and I sincerely hope that you will choose to take part in it. I, and my Secretariat, look forward to welcoming you all to Amity University, Noida in January, 2016, where you would witness a conference like no other!

Sincerely,
Pritika Kohli
Secretary General

The Committees

Reforming global diplomacy in an ever fragmented world.
UNGA - DISEC - The General Assembly (GA) is the main deliberative of the UN.
DISEC
The Economic and Finance Committee (ECOFIN) is a committee within the United Nations that solves problems in the area of global finances and economics.
ECOFIN
As its name suggests, the Social, Humanitarian Cultural Affairs Committee specializes in Social, Humanitarian and Cultural Issues. Much of its mandate concerns the examination of human rights questions, problems and crises.
SOCHUM
The Office of the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees was established on December 14, 1950 by the UN. The agency is mandated to lead and co-ordinate international action to protect refugees and resolve refugee problems worldwide.
UNHCR
The Peace and Security Council (PSC) is the African Union's (AU's) standing decision-making body responsible for the maintenance of continental peace and security.
AU-PSC
The Organisation of Islamic Cooperation is an international organisation founded in 1969 consisting of 57 member states. The organisation states that it is "the collective voice of the Muslim world" .
OIC
Under the Charter, the Security Council has primary responsibility for the maintenance of international peace and security. It has 15 Members, and each Member has one vote.
UNSC
